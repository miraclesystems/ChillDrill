About

Background

Chill Drills were developed by, and are presented by, Heidi J. Bauer, MSW, LCSW, a therapist who specializes in working with service members and their families. In her practice, she helps military service members who have come home from deployment with symptoms of stress.

About Military OneSource

The Chill Drills app is available through Military OneSource, a Department of Defense-funded program for service members, immediate family and survivors. Military OneSource is your 24/7 connection to information, answers and support for your best MilLife.

Military OneSource offers free solution-focused, non-medical counseling for a wide range of issues including couples’ communication, stress, loss, coping and more. This counseling is short-term, confidential and available at no cost to the military community. Sessions are conducted in person, by phone, online chat or video. You can reach a consultant at MilitaryOneSource.mil or by calling 800-342-9647.
